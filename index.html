ABOUT ME

My name is Ada, I was born in the beautiful city of Oaxaca. I lived in Guatemala where I studied to become an elementary teacher. Then I moved to Texas, and now I live in the mariachi land, Guadalajara.

I am an intrepid professional photographer who decided to make a 180-degree change, entering to a programming bootcamp. This was definitely a challenging experience, but at the same time, it was totally enriching. JavaScript was the chosen language to be the first one, it was totally interesting to discover many of its secrets, sometimes, it put me under pressure when I was trying to solve them, so I learned to be resilient. I found out that in order to learn JS, you also need a lot of creativity, just focused differently. In other words, learning a programming language is like a camera, you focus on what is important (the requirements you need to solve), capture the good time (the functions that actually worked), develop from the negative (all the mistakes you made in order to solve the problem), and if things don't work out, just take another shot (one more try, this is your code).
